## Goal

Invite pet merchants, pet shops, pet supply brands, groomers, boarding providers, breeders, transport partners, clinics and compliance partners to publish on PetGlobal.

Primary target first:
1. Pet shops and pet supply stores
2. Pet food, toy, clothing and accessory brands
3. Grooming, boarding and pickup service providers
4. Verified breeders and kennels
5. Pet transport, import/export and health certificate partners

## Positioning

PetGlobal is not a casual pet sales post. Position it as a verified marketplace and seller publishing platform with:

- Escrow payment
- Seller verification
- Vaccine, chip, health certificate and import/export file workflows
- Multilingual storefronts
- Pet supplies, grooming, boarding, pickup and live pet categories
- Seller wallet and payout workflows

Rules:
- Focus first on pet shops, pet supply brands, grooming providers, boarding providers and transport/compliance partners.
- Use public business websites, official directories, trade show exhibitor pages and public social profiles.
- Do not collect private personal data.
- Do not spam groups.
- Do not contact adoption/rescue communities unless business promotion is explicitly allowed.
- Log all targets in the outreach tracker.

Deliverable:

## Compliance Notes

- Do not claim PetGlobal guarantees animal welfare, licenses, delivery, or legal compliance unless verified.
- Do not imply live animals can be sold in countries where that is restricted.
- Use "verified sellers", "platform review" and "compliance file workflows" instead of unsupported guarantees.
- Respect each platform's rules and local anti-spam laws.
- For paid ads or broad email campaigns, add proper business sender details and unsubscribe instructions.
